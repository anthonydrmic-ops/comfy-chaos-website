SlideIQ — Digital Slide Map

Waterslide condition & maintenance management

A digital slide map and maintenance system that pins defects to their exact location on each waterslide. Issues are risk-scored on a 5×5 matrix, tracked over time through a workflow board, and rolled straight into a live 10-year capital expenditure plan — turning inspections and budgets into one connected picture for engineers and management.
